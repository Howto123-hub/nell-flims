Rhea's Journey

Drama • Action • Emotional    |   Rated 12+

In this film, STEM students make a short film about a happy-go-lucky student named Rhea.

As she takes on more responsibilities and pressure builds, Rhea begins to feel stressed and anxious. Her struggles reach a breaking point, but with help from loved ones, Rhea learns to cope and move forward. "Rhea's Journey" is a powerful and moving story about mental health and resilience.
